Lucerne

Canton of the Confederation since 1332. Political entity designated as Rät und Hundert der Stadt Luzern ( " Councils and Hundred of the City of Lucerne " ) until 1798, as " City and Republic " from 1814 to 1831, as " canton " of the proclamation of the Helvetic Republic (1798) to 1814 and since 1831. In German (official language) Luzern, in Italian and Romansch Lucerna. The capital is the city of the same name.
